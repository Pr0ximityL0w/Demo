* What we do - Services *

* Felling, Pruning, Removal, Clearance *

Whichever service you require be reassured to know that all work is carried out to the highest of standards, inline with NPTC (National Proficiency Tests Council) Certification and BS3998 (British Standard for Tree Work) regulations, we are fully insured (Through ArboRisk) and all our documents can be seen on request.

Being Qualified through the NPTC we are able to fell all manner of tree sizes starting from shrub and hedge, through Forestry and onto Emergency Operations, which covers catastrophic failure be that from decay or due to weather.

We are able to conduct climbing operations too, we understand the importance of trees and their place in our society and often in our customers hearts, there for we strive to offer every possible solution not just the Felling of Trees.

You and your tree may just require an health inspection, which being conducted airily can allow us to be the most thorough, we can spot potential hazards early be that disease or decay, fungal or faliure.

We provide pruning and shaping, lifting and reduction of the crown, dead wood removal and crown cleaning, aswell as reduction of the canopys density, allowing more light to pass through the canopy (This also allows wind through, which reduces the stress that can lead to failure), we are also able to conduct pollarding, this is primarily to maintain trees at a determined height.

Through Experience we can employ techniques which can allow us to safely and securely dismantle tree’s in tight places and even enter a canopy in order to recover pets.

Our tree surgeons have carried out their services for many people and sectors, from domestic and residential customers and areas through to the commercial and trade sectors.

We happily collaborate with local trade, from supply to gardeners, we are also proud to work for or alongside our local landscapers, both planning the project if required and conducting all necessary tree work and stump/root removal. All waste will be disposed of in a safe and ecologically friendly way, we strive to recycle and embrace our duties towards our enviroment and carbon foot print.
